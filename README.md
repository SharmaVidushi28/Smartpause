# SmartPause | Enterprise Subscription Dashboard

Today, most people use multiple subscription-based apps, including OTT platforms, learning tools, fitness apps, and cloud services. While subscribing is easy, keeping track of whether these services are actually being used is not. Many users forget to cancel or pause subscriptions even when they are no longer needed. Current finance apps mostly show a list of subscriptions and monthly spending, but they do not help users decide when to pause or cancel a subscription. This often leads to wasted money, subscription fatigue, and unnecessary digital usage.

Proposed Solution:
SmartPause is a dynamic subscription optimizer that helps users manage subscriptions in a smarter way. It analyzes bank and UPI transaction patterns to understand how users interact with their subscriptions over time. Based on this analysis, the system suggests actions such as pausing a subscription during low-usage periods, downgrading plans, or cancelling services that are no longer useful. The goal is to help users save money without affecting convenience.

How It Works:
	•	Identifies recurring payments from transaction history
	•	Groups transactions based on merchant name, amount, and billing cycle
	•	Analyzes usage patterns using factors such as recent activity, consistency of payments, cost, and historical gaps
	•	Categorizes subscriptions into groups like frequently used, occasionally used, or forgotten
	•	Provides simple recommendations along with estimated savings

SmartPause goes beyond basic subscription tracking by focusing on user behavior rather than just payment data. By suggesting actions at the right time, it helps users avoid unnecessary spending and build healthier digital habits. The solution has the potential to improve financial awareness while also promoting responsible digital consumption.

SmartPause helps users stay in control of their subscriptions instead of letting subscriptions control them. It's a group project contributed to UI design and the frontend.
